Why don't I see the price history widget?

Solution: Make sure you're on a product page (not search results). The URL should contain /dp/ or /gp/product/. Try refreshing the page if needed.

Does QuickPrice work on all Amazon sites?

Answer: Yes! QuickPrice works on:

  • Amazon.com (US)
  • Amazon.ca (Canada)
  • Amazon.co.uk (UK)
  • Amazon.de (Germany)
  • Amazon.fr (France)
  • Amazon.it (Italy)
  • Amazon.es (Spain)

How accurate is the price data?

Answer: QuickPrice analyzes 30 days of price history to show trends. While we strive for accuracy, prices can change rapidly. Always verify the current price before purchasing.

Is QuickPrice really free?

Answer: Yes! QuickPrice is completely free to use. No hidden costs, no premium version, no ads.
