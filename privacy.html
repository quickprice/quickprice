Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025
Simple Summary: QuickPrice does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All processing happens locally in your browser. We don't track you, period.

1. Information We DO NOT Collect

QuickPrice is designed with privacy first. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history or activity
  • Product views or purchases
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers
  • Any form of analytics or telemetry

2. How QuickPrice Works

QuickPrice operates entirely within your browser:

  • Price analysis happens locally on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No user accounts or registration required
  • Extension settings are stored locally in your browser

3. Permissions Used

QuickPrice requires minimal permissions:

  • Access to Amazon sites: To display price history on product pages
  • Storage: To save your preferences locally

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

QuickPrice may access public price history data to show trends. This is done anonymously without transmitting any user information.

5. Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there's nothing to secure on our end. All extension data remains local to your browser.

6. Children's Privacy

QuickPrice does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age, as it doesn't collect any user information at all.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how QuickPrice handles data, we will update this policy and notify users through the Chrome Web Store.

8. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data:

  • There's no data to request access to
  • There's no data to delete
  • There's no data to correct
  • You have complete privacy by default

9. Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy:

Email: quickprice.support@gmail.com

Note: QuickPrice is not affiliated with Amazon. We are an independent free tool designed to help consumers make informed purchasing decisions.
